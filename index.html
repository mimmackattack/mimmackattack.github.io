About

I'm a recent graduate of UC Davis with a master's degree in applied mathematics and a background in computer science and design. I earned my bachelor's in applied math from Tufts University in 2018.

I enjoy building mathematical visualizations and am a hobbyist web app designer. In my free time, I also love cooking with friends and writing speculative romances, and when I lived in California, I helped my parents build their own house in the Santa Cruz mountains.

Math Visualizations

  • How to Graph the p-Norm Unit Ball in 3D

    JavaScript Python How to graph a sphere, a cube, an octahedron, and every continuous shape in between with one function. A walk through the problem-solving process of graphing the p-norm unit ball in 3D using the Plotly graphing library.

  • Hopfield Network Simulation

    GitHub JavaScript simulation of a Hopfield network, a type of neural network that models associative memory.

  • Desmos Eigenvector Animation

    Simple, interactive animation built in Desmos for my linear algebra students. A lecture tool to illustrate the visual meaning of an eigenvector.
